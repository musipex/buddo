HISTORICAL BACKGROUND OF BUDDO SS.

The school opened its gates on 4/Feb/1991 with ten students in a humble setting. On Buddo Hillside, Kyengera town Council in Wakiso District.It was founded by profound, resilient and professional Educationists headed by Mr. Muwonge Lawrence under the guidance and chairmanship of Mr. Mujjumba John Bosco the Chairman Private Schools Association in Uganda then. Buddo Secondary School is a privately owned institution fully registered with the Ministry of Education Science Technology and Sports as a Boarding Mixed "O" And "A" Level Secondary School. In 2016 the School celebrated its Silver Jubilee and in the year 2021 we celebrated 30 years of providing Holistic Education in Uganda.
